Lets go down the river

Classic Rafting Adventure

Experience the thrill of navigating through exciting rapids and working together as a team to conquer the river. This adventure is perfect for those looking to bond with friends and older teen and adult family while enjoying the great outdoors. Our boats hold 8 rafters plus a guide and should be fully booked. If you need assistance filling out your part please contact us and we will work with you.

Family Rafting Adventure

Our family-friendly rafting trips are designed for rafters of all ages, including children as young as 6 years old. Enjoy a fun and safe adventure on the water, with gentle rapids and plenty of opportunities to spot wildlife and take in the stunning scenery. Our boats hold 8 rafters plus a guide but can launch on the calmer waters with as few as 6. If you need assistance filling out your part please contact usand we will work with you.

Solo Kayaking

Our solo kayaking trips are designed for experienced paddlers looking for a challenge. Navigate through exciting rapids and enjoy the tranquility of the river at your own pace. Our guides are available for support and safety, but this adventure is all about you. You won't be out there alone - we raft in minimums of 10 kayakers and two guides. It is rare to fall below our minimum. It is helpful if you bring a friend or two, but generally not necessary.
